About Me

Carpe Diem!

Besides pursuing my passion and work, I try to seize my days whenever possible!

In my adolescent years. I struggled to find a role model or anyone I can look up to, that could guide me along my journey. I would be envious of my peers who would tell stories of their siblings, relatives or parents and how much they have inspired, guided or provided for them.

Despite this and after graduating from secondary school and then attending ITE. My mindset of starting anew and openmindedness when i step foot into ITE, changed me in terms of how I look at things and my approach when it comes to work and life. I have found a great deal of satisfaction for learning, leading projects with my classmates and socializing. And only then, have I taken things into my own hands and gotten more confident to lead, guide, and act as my own role model.

I am an ambitious person, always in pursuit for something and I am keen on improving myself each day. I hold myself in high regards and I intend to contribute as much as I can to society while I am here!

In my free time I enjoy exploring film and music genres, listening to podcasts talking about philosophies or anything in general or having a prolonged conversations with my friends. I have only recently taken up interest in cooking and writing, I must say it's fun to look at recipes and fantasizing about preparing them. For now I am just going to stick with my salty burnt chicken breast and plain rice. I will update this if there are any developments in the cooking department.
