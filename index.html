Greenr

Sustainable Alternative E-Commerce utilizing AI

In this project, i scraped and annotated my own data with RPA and LabelStudio, then fine-tuned a pretrained BERT model to extract aspects and used those extracted aspects for aspect based sentiment analysis. I also used sentence-transformer to build a content based recommendation system that looks and compares on the similarity of a product based on its description.


The project was built using node js. I used tensorflow, and hugging face for the deep learning models. Afterwards i dockerized the models and deployed them to both azure and google cloud platform.

TutorHub

Build Your Skills With Tutors And learners Alike!

As one of the businesses which are affected greatly by Covid-19, Tuition businesses have to undergo digital transformation for a more reliable source of revenue. Why we say this is because tuition businesses often experience peaks and troughs in revenue. E.g. More income during exam periods, Less income during holiday seasons.

There are 4 Main Users: Admin, Tutee, Tutor, Institutions

  • It is a platform which brings together teachers and learners alike; All users who sign up will be a learner by default. Even after registering as a tutor, learning capabilities (registering for courses) are still applicable.
  • TutorHub operates by shifting tuition services online but not losing out on the interactive aspect. (Sessions will be conducted live to ensure lessons are interactive.)

It was initially developed with Flask, we have recently moved to using NodeJS, Express and MySQL

Learn more about this project by visiting my Github or checking out my Blog!

GrowBiz!

Digitally transform your business now!

As part of my Fundamentals of Innovation and Entreprise Project and my first introduction to Design thinking. I utilized empathy, definition, ideation, prototyping and testing to create a solution that could encourage SMEs in singapore to undergo a Digital Transformation process.

During the early stages of the COVID-19 pandemic, we saw many countries go into lockdown and business having to close down to abide by the regulations, however businesses that were quick adopters of the digital space found themselves in a fortunate situation. With most people and potential customers on their computing devices at home, we saw a huge spike in user traffic across different websites and applications. Armed with this knowledge and knowing they are still alot of businesses that are yet to adopt the digital space, we developed GrowBiz!

Through surveying, creating personas and plotting journey maps and pain points. We understand that businesses that have yet to adopt digital technology, are those whose management is reluctant or unsure of how digital technology might be beneficial. Therefore we developed GrowBiz that acts as a middleman to connect between traditional businesses and digital-services offering companies

Call For Help

Utilizing IBM Watson to reduce response time to emergencies.

With the increasingly aging population and a growing segment of vulnerable populations in mind, how might we leverage analytics for better sense-making to be alerted at the onset of incidents which require emergency response and mobilise CFRs for effective early intervention especially to the vulnerable population?

Therefore we will be making use of IBM Watson to craft an application to reduce response time when it comes to emergency situations(Medical or Fire) by alerting CFRs and professionals. And better prepare reponders with the right resources for the appropriate situation.
